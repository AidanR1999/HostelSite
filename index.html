Our Youth Hostel

About us!

The Hostel has one of the best locations in Scotland. From the attractive loch-side garden, the views across to mountain range are breathtaking. Good facilities mean the hostel is popular with walkers and tourist alike.

There are otters, badgers and pine martins in the area. Red deer are numerous, and on the mountains ravens, ptarmigan, golden eagles and mountain hares can be seen on occasion

... well situated for walking and exploring some of Scotland's most celebrated scenery"!

With no mobile phone reception the emphasis here is on tranquillity and relaxation!

Accommodation Information

What's Included?

Accommodation in shared dormitories: bunk, pillow, flat sheet and pillow case are provided. You will need to bring your own sleeping bag. Access to shared toilets and washing facilities. Access to self catering facilities. Basic cutlery, crockery and cooking utensils are provided. £9.50 per bed per night.

Accommodation in group rooms : bed with duvet and bed linen. Access to shared toilets, washing facilities. Access to self catering facilities. Basic cutlery, crockery and cooking utensils are provided. £11 per bed per night (min. group size 4, maximum 6.)

Accommodation in double rooms: double bed with duvet and bed linen. Access to shared toilets, washing facilities. Access to self catering facilities. Basic cutlery, crockery and cooking utensils are provided. £25 per room.

Purchasable Extras : Towel £4

(Guests can cancel their reservation free of charge up to 2 days before arrival. Deposits paid are non refundable.)
